Grilled Cheese

  • Time: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
Ingredients Quantity
Chesse (preferably cheddar) 2-3 Slices
Bread (any variety) 2 Slices
Butter As needed

Step 1: Obtain ingredients (duh).
Step 2: Set the heat to the pan to a medium (3-5) and butter the bread
Step 3: Place one slice of the bread butter side down and then place the cheese and the other piece of bread butter up
Step 4:Wait for one side to be golden brown then flip.
Enjoy!

Source: Old family tradition

Tomato Soup

  • Time: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Campbell's Condensed Tomato Soup 1 Can
Milk/water ***Use milk for creamyness 1 Canfull

Step 1: Obtain ingredients.
Step 2: Set the heat to a pot to a medium (4)
Step 3: Dump the contents of the can and fill it up with milk/water and then dump that into the pot
Step 4: Stir occasionally, making sure that large chunks are broken apart and it does not come to a boil.
Step 5: Wait about 5-10 minutes to heat up the soup, Do not let it boil
Enjoy!

Mr.C's World Famous Cobbler!

Note that this recipie is more improvised than planned

  • Time: 45-60 Minutes
  • Serves: 8 people
Ingredients Quantity
Apple Pie filling (homemade recommended) 2-3 Cans
Jiffy Yellow Cake Mix 2 9oz. Boxes
Butter 1 Stick, sliced

Step 1: Obtain ingredients (duh).
Step 2: Evenly spread the apple pie filling in a pan (any large enough size)
Step 3: Dump and spread the cake mix on the top
Step 4: Place the slices of butter across the top of the cake mix evenly spaced.
Step 5: Cook in the oven at 400 for 45 minutes or golden brown
Enjoy!
